Flashing Lights to Assist in Meditating

WARNING: If you have epilepsy, or someone you live with does, be very careful using this!

  • WARNING: If you have epilepsy, or someone you live with does, be very careful using this!
  • Press the blue start button to begin, but first read the directions!
  • Click anywhere or press a key to stop the flashing.
  • Press the F11 key on your keyboard to make it full-screen for better immersion (Windows only).
  • It works very well on smartphone if you turn the brightness all the way up and hold it up to your eyes with your eyes closed.
