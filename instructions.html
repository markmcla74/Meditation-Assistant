Instructions

  • Disable the "Adaptaptive Brightness" feature on your phone.
  • Adjust the brightness on your phone until you find what is most comfortable for you.
  • Temporarily disable notifications on your phone, so you won't be interrupted during your session.
  • Install an app that will keep your screen on. For example, install the Screen Alive App from the Google Play Store.
  • Connect your headphones to your smartphone.
  • Completely recline in your favorite recliner chair.
  • Press the blue start button to begin the flashing.
    TIP: Turn your phone horizontally, then scroll so you see the entire "Start" button.
    This will maximize the flashing part of the screen.
  • Put your smartphone in a "DIY Mind Machine Headset". This custom headset is described below.
    Alternatively, you could simply relax in a dark room and put a phone or tablet close to your face.
    A virtual reality headset can also work, although they are bulky.
  • Close your eyes.
  • Press anywhere on the screeen to stop the flashing.

Make a DIY Mind Machine Headset

For best results, you should make a "DIY Mind Machine Headset". There are three options for making this custom headset:

  • Option #1 (DIY approach)

    • Buy the following items from Amazon, and make your DIY Headset as shown in the following pictures
    • VR SHINECON Virtual Reality Headset
    • Elastic Bands for Wigs
    • Foam Face Replacement Face Cover Pad (optional)
    • Hacksaw, and Dremel Tool
    • Old plastic lunch tray, Machine screws, nuts, and washers.

  • Option #2 (3D printed approach)

    • Find someone who can 3D print these custom parts for the main body and front plate. Download the .stl files.
    • Purchase the Elastic Bands for Wigs and Foam Face Replacement Face Cover Pad
    • Put some velcro dots (prickly side) along the rim of the main body.
    • Attach the face cover to the rim of the main body. The face cover will stick to the dots.
    • Secure the straps to front plate. Secure the front plate to the main body using machine screws and washers.
